ABOUT US

Crafting Elegant Spaces With Premium Marble & Granite

Established in 1990, Prakash Marble And Granite has become one of the trusted marble and granite suppliers in Madanganj Kishangarh. Our commitment to premium quality products and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred destination for architects, builders and homeowners.

We specialize in Italian marble, granite, Korean stone and premium decorative surfaces that bring timeless beauty, durability and luxury to residential as well as commercial spaces.
